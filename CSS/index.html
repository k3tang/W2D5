Ingredients

  • 6 cups of hot water
  • 1 cup of organic cane sugar
  • 3 black tea bags
  • 1 Scoby culture
  • 1 cup of well-fermented kombucha (typically included with scoby)

Kombucha

Description

Bacteria and yeast byproducts that are great for your body.

Link to Wikipedia's Description of Kombucha

Directions

  1. Place sterilized glass container hot water, sugar, and tea.
  2. Allow sweetened tea mixture to come to room temperature.
  3. Once cooled, place scoby and fermented kombucha into sweetened tea mixture.
  4. Cover container opening with a clean cloth, secure with rubber band.
  5. Allow to ferment in a cool, dark corner for 3-4 weeks. Sip to taste.
  6. Once fermentation is done, may proceed to second fermentation with fruit, tea, and other flavor combinations. Otherwise, enjoy cold!
